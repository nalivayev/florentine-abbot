## 7. Risks and Mitigation

### 7.1 Technical Risks

**ExifTool Dependency**
- Risk: Critical dependency on single-maintainer tool
- Mitigation: Alternative libraries, financial support, fork if needed

**Technology Obsolescence**
- Risk: Python, file formats may become outdated
- Mitigation: Standards adherence, regular updates

**Scalability**
- Risk: Archives may reach terabytes
- Mitigation: Performance optimization, incremental processing

### 7.2 Market Risks

**Competition**
- Risk: Adobe, Google may add archival features
- Mitigation: Focus on standards, openness, niche positioning

**User Preferences**
- Risk: Preference shift to cloud solutions
- Mitigation: Hybrid approach, self-hosted web, data control education

### 7.3 Organizational Risks

**Bus Factor**
- Risk: Dependence on single maintainer
- Mitigation: Documentation, core contributor team, succession planning

**Burnout**
- Risk: Maintainer burnout
- Mitigation: Funding for full-time work, team distribution
